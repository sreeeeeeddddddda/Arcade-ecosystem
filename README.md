#Built With
HTML5 / CSS3
JavaScript (ES6+)
Three.js (WebGL 3D Library)
3D Visualizations: Rendered using Three.js with dynamic lighting, fog, and particle systems (rain).

Grid-Based Ecosystem:
🌱 Plants (Flora): Automatically grow based on climate conditions.
🐇 Herbivores: Roam the grid, hunt plants for energy, and reproduce.
🐺 Predators: Hunt herbivores, consume them for energy, and replicate.
Interactive Tools: Click and drag on the 3D plane to manually place plants, animals, or drop meteors.
Weather System: Toggle between Sunny and Rainy weather to alter plant growth rates and atmospheric effects.
Disasters & Disease:
🏜️ Drought: Clears a large percentage of plant life.
☣️ Virus Outbreak: Infects entities, causing faster energy depletion and contagiousness on contact.
Real-time Telemetry: Live counters tracking population levels and infection spread.
